## 7-PHASE PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

```
PHASE 1: IDEATION & VALIDATION (15-60 min)
├─ Niche/topic discovery (VidIQ, Perplexity)
├─ Viral potential assessment (STEPPS framework)
└─ GATE 1: Strategic Validation

PHASE 2: RESEARCH & SCRIPTING (60-80 min)
├─ Research stream (Perplexity + Fabric)
├─ Script outlining & drafting (Claude)
└─ GATE 2: Coverage Check / GATE 3: Completeness

PHASE 3: VERIFICATION & REFINEMENT (30-40 min)
├─ Fact-checking (Originality.ai)
├─ Polish & optimization (Claude)
└─ GATE 4: Fact Integrity & Readiness

PHASE 4: VISUAL PRODUCTION (25-35 min)
├─ Thumbnail design (Canva, 3 variants)
├─ B-roll sourcing (stock + AI generation)
└─ Animation creation (After Effects / Fusion)

PHASE 5: AUDIO PRODUCTION (20-30 min)
├─ Voice generation (ElevenLabs)
├─ Post-processing (DAW: Reaper/Audition)
└─ Music selection & mixing (Epidemic Sound)

PHASE 6: EDITING & POST-PRODUCTION (30-45 min)
├─ Video assembly (DaVinci Resolve)
├─ Pacing optimization (15-25 cuts/min)
└─ Pattern interrupts (every 60-90s)

PHASE 7: PUBLISHING & DISTRIBUTION (15-25 min)
├─ Metadata optimization (title/description/tags)
├─ YouTube upload + end screens
└─ Multi-platform adaptation (TikTok/IG)

TOTAL TIME: 3-4 hours (AI-augmented) vs. 8 hours (manual)
TIME SAVINGS: 60-70%
```

## KEY DECISION MATRICES

### When to Scale Team
| Trigger | Action | Expected Outcome |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| Solo working >15 hrs/wk | Hire Editor/Assistant | Free 5-8 hrs for strategy |
| 2-person team at capacity | Add 2nd Editor OR Researcher | Increase to 5-6 videos/wk |
| CTR/AVD declining >10% | Hire Graphic Designer | Restore quality, +15-20% CTR |
| Revenue >$5K/month | Reinvest 30-40% in team | Compound growth |

### Tool Selection Priorities
1. **Bootstrap ($0-50/mo):** Perplexity + Claude + ElevenLabs Starter + Free tools
2. **Professional ($100-200/mo):** Add Canva Pro, Epidemic, Originality.ai, VidIQ
3. **Production Team ($300-500/mo):** Full stack (After Effects, Storyblocks, ChatGPT Team)

### Quality vs. Quantity Trade-off
- **80/20 Rule:** 80% quality at 200% volume > 100% quality at 100% volume
- **Red Flags:** CTR <3%, AVD <30%, 30s retention <60% → STOP SCALING, FIX QUALITY
- **Acceptable Range:** 80-95/100 quality, optimize for 85/100 at max sustainable volume

## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS (Top 10)

1. **Strategic Foundation First:** 5-10 hours on niche validation prevents 200+ hours wasted
2. **AI Augments, Doesn't Replace:** Human decisions at all CRITICAL points (see Section 7)
3. **Quality Gates Non-Negotiable:** Run all 4 gates (Strategic, Coverage, Completeness, Fact Integrity)
4. **Reusable Assets = ROI:** Templates save 10-25 min/use × infinite reuse
5. **Retention > Volume:** Algorithm rewards retention, not quantity
6. **Multi-Platform Funnel:** YouTube 80% effort, TikTok/IG 15% (traffic funnel)
7. **Document Before Scaling:** SOPs required before hiring (8hr onboarding vs. 40hr chaos)
8. **Analytics Drive Iteration:** A/B test, measure, optimize (not guesswork)
9. **Sustainability > Short-term CPM:** $5 CPM sustained (24mo) > $20 CPM abandoned (3mo)
10. **First 15 Seconds = 80% Success:** Hook window critical (75-85% retention target)

---

## COMMON PITFALLS (Avoid These)

**Strategic:** Skipping niche validation, chasing trends without evergreen, premature pivoting
**Production:** Weak hooks, inconsistent audio, overcomplex thumbnails, no pattern interrupts
**Workflow:** No Planning Files, skipping gates, manual tasks AI can do, no session logs
**Scaling:** Hiring before capacity maxed, volume increase while quality declining, no SOPs
**Analytics:** Ignoring retention curves, no A/B testing, random publish times
